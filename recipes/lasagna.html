Lasagna

A recipe for lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasoning
  • Lasagne Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagne according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. let the lasagna rest before serving
